# 🧠 The Addict’s Agenda
*A Progressive Web App (PWA) for Recovery, Reflection & Growth*

---

## 🌱 Overview
**The Addict’s Agenda** is an all-in-one digital recovery companion designed to help you manage cravings, reflect daily, and stay organized on your personal growth journey.

Built as a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**, it runs seamlessly on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop — and even works **offline** once installed.

---

## 🧩 Features

### 💬 Coping Cards
A deck of **52 interactive coping cards** for cravings — offering practical strategies, affirmations, and grounding exercises to help you navigate difficult moments.

### 📝 Daily Journal
A secure, local journaling tool for reflections, gratitude entries, or step work.  
Data is stored in your browser’s **LocalStorage** for complete privacy — nothing ever leaves your device.

### ✅ To-Do List
A simple and effective task manager to help you structure your day, track commitments, and maintain accountability.

### 📚 Literature
Access to **recovery readings**, 12-Step literature, and inspirational passages to strengthen your program and mindset.

### 📘 Workbooks
Interactive **12-Step workbooks** (Steps 1 through 12), each with guided questions and space for journaling — perfect for working the steps digitally.

---

## ⚙️ Technical Details
- Built with **HTML**, **CSS**, and **vanilla JavaScript**
- **PWA-enabled** with `manifest.json` + `service worker` (`sw.js`)
- Uses **LocalStorage** for data persistence
- Fully **responsive** (mobile-friendly)
- Works best over **HTTPS** or a local development server
